The Story Behind Sandy's Sweet Treats

Welcome to Sandy’s Sweet Treats, where every bite carries a story.

Founded by Sandile Mola, a passionate 26-year-old self-taught baker from the rural heart of Bushbuckridge, Mpumalanga, now based in Gauteng.

Baking started as more than just learning a skill. It became a special way for Sandile to connect with his late mother, who always found joy in his early baking “mistakes” while he taught himself the craft.

What began as a way to support his student journey slowly grew into something bigger, a mission to share comfort, happiness, and a little sweetness with every creation.

“At Sandy’s Sweet Treats, every cake, pastry and dessert is made with care, resilience and love.”

Sandy’s Sweet Treats — Baked with love, shared with joy!
