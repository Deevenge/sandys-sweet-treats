About Sandy

Welcome to Sandy's Sweet Treats! Founded by Sandile Mola, a passionate baker from South Africa, our goal is simple: to bring a little sweetness to your day.

From soft, buttery scones to crunchy, delicious cookies and fluffy muffins, every treat is handmade with love and the finest ingredients.

Whether it’s a cozy breakfast, a midday snack, or sharing with friends and family, Sandy's Sweet Treats is all about creating moments of joy through baked goodness.

Follow us on TikTok to see our treats in action and stay updated on new flavors and specials! Every bite is a little celebration.
